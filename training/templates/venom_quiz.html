1. What should you do first if bitten by a snake?

Stay calm and call emergency services
Apply ice to the bite area
Try to suck out the venom

2. How should you treat a spider bite?

Clean the bite, apply a cool compress, and seek help if symptoms worsen
Apply ice directly to the wound and avoid moving the affected area
Apply pressure to stop bleeding and apply heat

3. What is the first step if stung by an insect?

Clean the sting site and apply a cool compress
Apply a tourniquet above the sting site
Remove the stinger (if visible), clean the site

4. How should you treat a scorpion sting?

Apply pressure to stop venom spread
Clean the sting, apply ice, and seek medical help for severe reactions
Wash the area with hot water

5. What should you do in a remote area if you get bitten or stung?

Immobilize the limb, clean wounds, and signal for help
Walk to the nearest hospital without waiting
Apply a tourniquet and wait for the venom to subside
