1. What should you do if someone has ingested poison?

Induce vomiting immediately
Call emergency services and provide water or milk if advised

2. How should you assist a person with inhalation poisoning?

Get them to fresh air and call emergency services
Let them rest and give them water
Try to make them vomit

3. What is the first step in dealing with contact poisoning (e.g., chemical exposure)?

Wash the affected area with soap and water
Apply an ointment or cream
Apply ice or heat to the affected area

4. If the person is unconscious due to poisoning, what should you do?

Call emergency services and monitor their breathing
Leave them and wait for help
Try to make them drink water

5. How do you handle a poisoning case where the substance is unknown?

Call emergency services immediately and provide any available information
Try to treat them with home remedies
Wait to see if symptoms worsen
